FOCO Model

The FOCO model allows investors to own the vending machine asset while NexGen Vendors manages the complete operations.

This structure combines asset ownership with professionally managed operations.

Step 01

Location Identification

NexGen identifies suitable locations such as colleges, hospitals, hostels, and offices for vending machine placement.

Step 02

Machine Deployment

NexGen provides high-quality vending machines along with complete service, maintenance, and technical support.

Step 03

Warehouse Management

NexGen manages product storage and inventory through its warehouse system to ensure a consistent supply for vending machines.

Step 04

Logistics & Transportation

NexGen manages the transportation of products from the warehouse to vending machine locations.

Step 05

Machine Refill

NexGen regularly refills the vending machines to ensure products are always available for customers.
